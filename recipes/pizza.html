Pizza

To make a delicious homemade pizza, you'll need the right ingredients and a bit of patience. Here's a brief overview of the recipe:

Ingrediants

  • 2½ cups organic all-purpose flour (300 grams) or (1 cup all-purpose flour & 1½ cup wheat flour)
  • 1 teaspoon dry active yeast or instant yeast (3 grams) (or ⅓ tsp for slow rise)
  • ½ cup warm water +2 tbsp. (or as needed)
  • 1½ tablespoon olive oil (or any oil)
  • ⅓ teaspoon salt (use as needed)
  • 1 teaspoon organic sugar (or 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup)
  • 1 tablespoon cornmeal (or fine semolina)
  • Pizza sauces
  • Pizza toppings

Instructions

  1. Make pizza dough by mixing yeast, flour, salt, and oil in a bowl. Knead the dough for 5-6 minutes until it becomes soft and elastic.
  2. Proof the dough at room temperature until it doubles or triples in size.
  3. Prepare pizza sauce by sautéing garlic, red chili flakes, and herbs in oil. Add tomatoes, salt, and sugar, and cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Assemble the pizza by spreading sauce, layering cheese and vegetables, and sprinkling herbs and chili flakes.
  5. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at 220-240°C (470F) for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
